The Kingdom of Elenoir is the elven kingdom deep in the Forest of Elshire of Dicathen. Located on the northen border of the continent, the kingdom is primarily composed of elves.

The Kingdom of Elenoir is established deep within the Forest of Elshire of Dicathen. It is located on the northern border of the continent where a dense array of branches that fan out from the trees cover most of the city in a canopy of shade. The capital of the Kingdom of Elenoir is the City of Zestier, where the royal family resides. The capital is filled with numerous floating orbs of light surrounding the city, creating a warm, luminescent glow. Buildings are made from jade-like material intertwined by large, tall trees, and homes are built on unnaturally thick branches extending out of massive trunks. The elven city is covered in a lush field of soft moss, with only narrow sidewalks and the main road paved with smooth stone. Within the City of Zestier, a large enormous tree made of white stone inhabiting the home of the royal family. The interior of the castle had two curved staircases that created a circle, with a grand chandelier made from the same orbs of light that surrounded the city, floating in the center.

The Kingdom of Elenoir is a monarchy, which was ruled by Alduin Eralith prior to his death.
Elven Royalty Elven royalty is dressed in fine clothing with golden accessories across their attire, signifying their wealth. Both the king and queen wear golden leaflet crowns on top of their heads.
Elven Warriors Elven warriors guard the gates to the Kingdom of Elenoir and are dressed in coordinated black suits with green trimmings and a golden shoulder guard on their left shoulder. They carry a golden rapier strapped to their waist along with a black sash hung across their right shoulder. The elven warriors wear black fingerless gloves along with thick black armor hidden beneath their black buttoned up cloak.

The Kingdom of Sapin is the largest and most populated region of Dicathen. Located on the eastern border of the continent (western as seen in illustrations), the kingdom is primarily composed of humans, but there are also numerous merchants from the Dwarven race, trading many commodities. Moreover, Sapin has a symbiotic relationship with the Kingdom of Darv when it comes to resources.
